OUR SERVICES

Pro-tier glove care services - tailored to your exact needs

Full Restoration

Complete overhaul: relacing, repairs, deep cleaning, conditioning, and refinishing. Bring vintage gloves back to life.

Process: Deep clean, repair all damage, complete relace, condition leather, restore color and finish

Turnaround: 5-7 business days

Full Re-lace

Complete glove relacing with your choice of 50+ premium leather lace colors. Includes thorough inspection and minor repairs.

Process: Remove all old lacing, inspect for damage, repair worn areas, install new laces with professional technique

Turnaround: 3-5 business days

Order Now

Lace Repair

Professional repair of broken or worn laces. We replace damaged sections and restore your glove's structure and integrity.

Process: Identify damaged laces, remove and replace broken sections, match existing lace color, tighten and adjust

Turnaround: 2-3 business days

Order Now

Cleaning/Conditioning

Deep cleaning and professional conditioning to restore leather flexibility, remove dirt and grime, and extend glove life.

Process: Deep clean with leather-safe products, apply premium conditioner, restore suppleness, protect against cracking

Turnaround: 3-4 business days

Order Now

Break-in Service

Professional break-in and conditioning service. We shape and soften your glove to game-ready perfection using proven techniques.

Process: Steam treatment, professional pounding and shaping, pocket formation, conditioning, game-ready preparation

Turnaround: 4-6 business days

Order Now

Leather Repair

Expert repair of tears, worn areas, and damaged leather. We restore your glove's integrity with quality leather patches and expert stitching.

Process: Assess damage, source matching leather, reinforce weak areas, professional stitching, blend and color-match

Turnaround: 4-5 business days
